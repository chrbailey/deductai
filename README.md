# DeductAI

**AI that finds tax deductions freelancers forget.**

Freelancers miss thousands in deductions every year because tax rules are confusing and scattered. DeductAI analyzes expenses and identifies potential write-offs automatically.

---

## What it does

DeductAI helps freelancers:

• Discover overlooked tax deductions  
• Categorize expenses automatically  
• Understand deduction rules in plain English  
• Estimate potential tax savings  

Instead of digging through IRS publications, DeductAI highlights deductions instantly.

---

## Example

A freelancer uploads expenses:

• Laptop purchase  
• Home office utilities  
• Software subscriptions  
• Mileage for client work  

DeductAI detects possible deductions such as:

• Section 179 equipment deduction  
• Home office percentage deduction  
• Business software expenses  
• Mileage deduction  

Then it explains **why the deduction qualifies**.

---

## Who this is for

Freelancers and independent workers including:

• Developers  
• Designers  
• Consultants  
• Writers  
• Creators  
• Gig workers  

Anyone filing **Schedule C income**.

---

## Why this matters

Most freelancers either:

• Miss deductions  
• Overpay taxes  
• Or spend hours researching tax rules  

DeductAI reduces that friction by surfacing deductions automatically.
