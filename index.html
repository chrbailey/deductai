Questions

 

Is my bank data safe?

We use Plaid, the same service used by Venmo, Robinhood, and Coinbase. We never see your bank login credentials. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

How accurate is the AI?

Our AI uses Claude to classify transactions against IRS Publication 535 and Schedule C rules. High-confidence deductions are auto-classified; edge cases are flagged for your review. You always have final say.

Does this replace my accountant?

No. DeductAI finds deductions your accountant might miss because they don't see every transaction. Export the results to your CPA or import into TurboTax Self-Employed. Think of it as a second pair of eyes.

What types of freelancers is this for?

Anyone filing Schedule C: freelance developers, designers, writers, consultants, rideshare drivers, real estate agents, photographers, coaches — if you're self-employed and have business expenses, this is for you.

How much does it cost?

Early access is free. We're building this with real users first. Our target pricing is $20/month — less than 1% of the average savings we find.
